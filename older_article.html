Adopting a defensive mechanism to reduce cyberbullying amidst students.

Cyberbullying can be briefly defined as “sending or posting harmful or cruel text or images using the Internet or other digital communication devices”.

Cyberbullying involves the use of information and communication technologies, such as e-mail, cell phone and pager text messages, instant messaging, defamatory personal Web sites, and defamatory online personal polling Web sites, to support deliberate, repeated, and hostile behavior by an individual or group that is intended to harm others

Living a purposeful life with emotional intelligence.

Living a purposeful life is living a life with a difference. Amit ray said “It does not matter how long you are spending on the earth, how much money you have gathered or how much attention you have received. It is the amount of positive vibration you have radiated in life that matters,”

Purposeful modification of the self only commences with revising your mind’s ability to recognize, understand, manage and express your internal functions (Emotions) to positively affect your real life (Purpose). Living a purposeful life is living a life of your own purpose and dream, not a copy and paste of someone you like or respect; else you will end up unhappy and unfulfilled. The first step in regulating and managing emotions is learning to identify and label them as they occur. This ability often requires not only the ability to observe one’s emotional responses in situations but also to describe with a sense of accuracy the context in which the emotions occur.

Emotional Management Strategy.

Emotional management been the ability to realize, readily accept, as well as successfully control feelings in oneself and having a complete authority over changes in one’s thoughts and feelings that are generated whenever one’s values are touched by the actions of a person or an event.
